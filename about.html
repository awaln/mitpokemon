About Us

Who We Are

Since 1996, Pokémon has spawned six generations of video games, an animated TV series, and its own trading card game. Many of us have been Pokémon fans since childhood, but we welcome everybody who is interested in the franchise!

The MIT Pokémon League exists to promote an MIT student community that plays and enjoys Pokémon together. Still, MITPL is open to the public as well as the general MIT community.

Come play with us!

What We Do

At League meetings, you can play matches, trade Pokémon, or just hang around and chat about Pokémon with fellow students and fans. Drop in anytime and leave when you need to!

You’ll also get a stamp card that you can fill by playing VG or TCG matches if you’re feeling competitive. A win or a loss will net you a stamp, and filling the rows will earn you prizes! At the end of the season, you’ll get Play! Points that can qualify you for tournaments. (See the official Play! Pokémon website for details.)
